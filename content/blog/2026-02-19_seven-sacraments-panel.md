Dear St. Mary Parishioners,

The **Cultural Committee** cordially invites you to the first session of a new Question & Answer Panel Series focused on the **Seven Sacraments of the Armenian Church**.

### Event Details

- **Topic:** Baptism
- **Date:** Sunday, February 22, 2026
- **Time:** 1:00 PM (following Badarak)
- **Location:** Stambolian Hall, St. Mary Armenian Apostolic Church

### Speakers

The discussion will be led by **Hayk Madoyan** and **Hayr Yeremia Abgaryan**, offering participants an opportunity to deepen their understanding of the sacramental life within the Armenian Apostolic Church through an open, welcoming format.

### Logistics

- Light luncheon will be provided
- Free admission
- All parishioners welcome

The Cultural Committee encourages broad participation in this new educational series. We look forward to seeing you there!

*St. Mary Armenian Apostolic Church of Costa Mesa*
