---
title: 'St. Mary Celebrated Christmas 2013'
date: '2013-01-08'
author: 'admin'
category: 'Parish News'
---

The sancuary, hallways and doorways of the St. Mary Armenian Church of Costa Mesa was packed with young and old faitful as we celebrated the birth of Christ On January 6th. After a devine ceremony conducted by Arch. Priest Fr. Moushegh Tashjian an abundant luncheon we served in the Fesjian Hall. More that 200 parisheners were accomodated as the day went into history as one of the most crowded Christmas Celebrations of St. Mary.
