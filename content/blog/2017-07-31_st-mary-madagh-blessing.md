<p>St. Mary Armenian Apostolic Church of Costa Mesa invites parishioners and friends to the Annual Madagh Blessing on Sunday, August 13, 2017. The Madagh is a cherished Armenian tradition of thanksgiving and communal sharing, featuring a blessing of meat and grapes by the parish priest, followed by a fellowship meal. All are welcome.</p>
