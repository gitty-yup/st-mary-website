*"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to Him, and He will make your paths straight."*
*(Proverbs 3:5–6)*

Dear Faithful,

Sometimes in life we want to understand everything. We want clear answers, clear plans, and clear results. But God teaches us something deeper — trust.

Trust means believing in God even when you don't see the full picture. It means walking forward when the road is uncertain, knowing that God is guiding you. When you rely only on your own understanding, you can feel worried, confused, or afraid. But when you place your heart in God's hands, He gives peace.

God promises that if you acknowledge Him in every part of your life — your family, your work, your health, your decisions — He will direct your path. You are not walking alone. Even when you feel lost, God is leading you step by step.

Tonight, take a moment to pray and say: *"Lord, I trust You. Guide my steps."*

May God give you peace, strength, and clear direction in your life.

### Հայերեն

*«Ամբողջ սրտով վստահիր Տիրոջը, և մի՛ հենվիր քո սեփական բանականությանը. Ճանաչիր Նրան քո բոլոր ճանապարհներում, և Նա ուղիղ կդարձնի քո ճանապարհները»:*
*(Առակաց 3:5-6)*

Թող Աստված ձեզ պարգևի խաղաղություն, ուժ և հստակ ուղղություն ձեր կյանքում:

Prayerfully,
**Very Rev. Fr. Yeremia Abgaryan**
