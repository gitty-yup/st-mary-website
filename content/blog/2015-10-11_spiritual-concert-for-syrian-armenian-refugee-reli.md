title: 'Spiritual Concert for Syrian Armenian Refugee Relief'
date: '2015-10-11'
author: 'admin'

---

<p>St. Mary Armenian Church of Costa Mesa hosted a spiritual concert in support of Syrian Armenian refugee relief efforts. The event brought together the parish community and friends to raise awareness and funds for Armenian families displaced by the Syrian conflict.</p>
