<p>St. Mary Armenian Church of Costa Mesa was pleased to present a program honoring the life and legacy of Komitas Vardapet — the visionary Armenian composer, musicologist, and priest who collected and preserved thousands of Armenian folk songs and sacred liturgical works. His contributions remain foundational to Armenian music to this day.</p>
