**Krisdos dzunav yev haydnetsav!** (Christ is born and revealed!)
**Orhnyal e haytnutyunn Krisdosi!** (Blessed is the Revelation of Christ!)

Dear Parish Family,

As we approach the sacred celebration of the Nativity and Theophany of our Lord Jesus Christ, we warmly invite you and your families to join us at St. Mary's Armenian Church for our Armenian Christmas services. This joyous feast reminds us of God's infinite love revealed through the birth and baptism of His Son, bringing light, hope, and renewal to our lives.

Please mark your calendars and come together in prayer and fellowship as we commemorate this holy occasion.

---

### St. Mary's Armenian Christmas Services Schedule

**Armenian Christmas Eve (Jurakalouyts)**
Monday, January 5, 2026

- 5:00 PM – Reading of Scripture
- 5:30 PM – Divine Liturgy

**Armenian Christmas Day**
Tuesday, January 6, 2026

- 10:00 AM – Morning Service
- 10:30 AM – Divine Liturgy
- 12:00 PM – Blessing of Water, commemorating the Baptism of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ

---

Immediately following the church services, all parishioners are warmly invited to a **Christmas Luncheon**, graciously hosted by the **St. Mary Ladies Society**.

We look forward to sharing this blessed time with you, strengthening our community in faith and tradition. Bring your loved ones and invite friends to experience the beauty of our Armenian Apostolic heritage.

May the light of Christ illuminate your hearts and homes this Christmas season.

With prayers and blessings,
**St. Mary Parish Council**
