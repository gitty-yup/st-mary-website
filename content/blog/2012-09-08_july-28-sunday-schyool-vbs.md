---
title: 'Sunday School Vacation Bible School Sleepover'
date: '2012-09-08'
author: 'admin'
category: 'Parish News'
---

<p>The Sunday School organized a Vacation Bible School with a sleepover at the church. The program centered on the creation story of the Bible — "Adam and Eve" — which was tied to an Armenian wedding, with children acting as the groom, bride, best man, maid of honor, parents, and guests. Archpriest Fr. Moushegh Tashjian, parents, and staff all participated in the instructions and activities. The next morning, a nature walk was held at the Back Bay. Everyone returned to the church for service and received Holy Communion.</p>
