---

<hr />

The Sunday School organized a Vacation Bible School with sleepover at the church. The program included the creation story of the Bible, "Adam and Eve" which was tied to an "Armenian wedding", children acting as "pesa" (Groom) and "Hars" (Bride), "Gnkahayr" (Bestman) and "Gnkamayr" (Maid of Honor), "parents" and "guests". Archpriest Fr. Moushegh Tashjian Pastor and parents and staff participated in the instructions and activities. The next morning, a nature walk was held at the Back Bay. Everyone returned to church for service and received Holy Communion.
