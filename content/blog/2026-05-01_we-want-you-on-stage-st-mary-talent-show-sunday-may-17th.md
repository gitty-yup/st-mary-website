Get ready for an unforgettable afternoon of talent, laughter, and community spirit!

Hello St. Mary Parishioners and Friends!

The Cultural Committee is excited to host our **Talent Show** on **Sunday, May 17th, 2026**, and we're reaching out to encourage **you** to step forward and share your talent with our church community!

This is a wonderful opportunity to shine, celebrate our gifts, and strengthen the bonds of our parish family. Whether you sing, dance, play an instrument, recite poetry, do magic, tell jokes, or have any other hidden talent — we want to see it! All ages are welcome to perform.

## Performance Guidelines

- Each act must be **3 minutes or less**
- Please include the title/piece and a brief description when you sign up

## Event Details

- 📅 **Date & Time:** Sunday, May 17th, 2026 at **1:15 PM**
- 📍 **Location:** St. Mary Armenian Church – Stambolian Hall

We'll have delicious food, great fellowship, and a supportive audience cheering you on. Come perform and enjoy the show as well!
