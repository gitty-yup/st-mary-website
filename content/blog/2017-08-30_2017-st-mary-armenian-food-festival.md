<strong>St. Mary Armenian Church- Costa Mesa to Hold 9<sup>th</sup> Annual Festival</strong>

This year St. Mary Armenian Apostolic Church in Costa Mesa will hold its 9<sup>th</sup> Annual Armenian Festival on Saturday October 7<sup>th</sup> 4-10 PM and Sunday, October 8<sup>th</sup> Noon-6 PM. The festival is held on the church grounds, located at 148 E. 22<sup>nd</sup> Street and admission is $3.

Come and enjoy delicious homemade Armenian foods like shish kebab, rice pilaf, hummus, and stuffed grape leaves, and leave some room for our fantastic desserts like baklava to enjoy with a cup of Armenian coffee. We also have a large assortment of vegetarian options. All while listening to live Armenian music, all day, both days! We have the Hosharian Brothers Band, to get up and line dance to or perhaps you want to watch some groups doing traditional line dances. Maybe you will want to treat yourself by listening to the ancient and traditional music of the duduk, played by Albert Vardanian, or the kanoon played by Lilit Khojayan. Dinner and a show!

There is a variety of vendors for shopping, including cultural items, books, &amp; CDs, a kids’ area, 50/50 cash raffle and it just keeps going! So, come out and join us to learn a little about our Armenian culture, take a tour of our Armenian church, and above all, have a fun filled day! You won’t want to miss out on this once a year, exciting event!

For more information, please contact (949) 432- MARY (6279) or visit the festival website: <a href="http://www.stmaryarmenianfestival.com/">www.stmaryarmenianfestival.com</a>
