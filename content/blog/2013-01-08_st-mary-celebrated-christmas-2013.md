---
title: 'St. Mary Celebrated Christmas 2013'
date: '2013-01-08'
author: 'admin'
category: 'Parish News'
---

<p>The sanctuary, hallways, and doorways of St. Mary Armenian Church of Costa Mesa were packed with young and old faithful as we celebrated the birth of Christ on January 6th. After a divine ceremony conducted by Archpriest Fr. Moushegh Tashjian, an abundant luncheon was served in the Fesjian Hall. More than 200 parishioners were accommodated as the day went into history as one of the most crowded Christmas celebrations in St. Mary's history.</p>
