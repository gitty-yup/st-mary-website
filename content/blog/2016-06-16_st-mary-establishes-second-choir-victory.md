date: '2016-06-16'

<p>St. Mary Armenian Apostolic Church of Costa Mesa is pleased to announce the establishment of a second parish choir, <em>Glory</em>. This new ensemble joins the beloved Hovsepian Choir in enriching our Divine Liturgy and parish life with sacred Armenian music. All parishioners with a love of music and worship are warmly encouraged to participate.</p>
