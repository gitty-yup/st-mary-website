**🎉 You're Invited! St. Mary Cultural Committee Talent Show 🎉**

  
Hello everyone!

Get ready for an unforgettable afternoon of talent, laughter, and community spirit! The Cultural Committee is thrilled to host our **Talent Show** on **Sunday, May 17th, 2026**, and **everyone is invited**!

All ages are welcome to attend and perform. Whether you sing, dance, play an instrument, recite poetry, do magic, or showcase any other hidden gift — this is your moment to shine!

Join us for a joyful celebration filled with amazing performances, delicious food, warm fellowship, and plenty of fun. We’d love to see our St. Mary parishioners come together and share their incredible talents with one another in the beautiful Stambolian Hall.

**Performance Guidelines:**

-   Each act must be **3 minutes or less**
-   Please include the title/piece and a brief description of your performance when signing up

**Event Details:**   
📅 **Date & Time:** Sunday, May 17th, 2026 at **1:15 PM**  
📍 **Location:** St. Mary Armenian Church – Stambolian Hall  
148 22nd Street, Costa Mesa, CA 92627

Ready to participate? We can’t wait to see what you’ve got!  
Sign up today through the link or QR code below.

For questions, contact **Nazeli** at **714-319-9101**.

Let’s make this an afternoon to remember — full of talent, joy, and church family love!

With excitement and love,  
**St. Mary Cultural Committee**
