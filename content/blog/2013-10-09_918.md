<p style="text-align: center;"> <span style="font-size: xx-large;"><strong>2013 FESTIVAL WAS A GREAT SUCCESS</strong></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Our 5th annual Festival held Saturday and Sunday, October 5th and 6th, was not only a </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">tremendous success, but was also a fun and memorable time for families and friends. </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Many had the pleasure to reunite with long-time friends and relatives they had not seen </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">in years.</span></span></p>

<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;"></span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Approximately 3,000+ guests attended the standing-room-only, weekend long event. </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Seating for 500 was provided under the shade of a 4,000 square foot lighted canopy. </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Additional large, lighted canopies for dancing, beer and wine garden, and pastries, </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">created a beautiful, friendly atmosphere. There was also additional seating for 100 at </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">umbrella tables.</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">We had mouth-watering beef, chicken and luleh kebabs, yalanchi, boreg, kufta, </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">lahmajoon, shawarma sandwich, vegetarian dishes, scrumptious desserts, Armenian </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">coffee, and a beer and wine garden. Beautiful, traditional Armenian folk music was</span></span>
<span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">provided by Tom Bozigian, who also gave dance lessons. Our own folk dance group </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">performed for the crowd both days.</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Many not familiar with our faith had the opportunity to attend church tours offered twice </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">daily by Fr. Moushegh.</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">This year, we offered attendees the first edition of our Official Souvenir Program, </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">complete with information about Festival activities, a little bit about Armenian history and </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">traditions, beautiful photographs, and ads from our well-wishers and businesses.</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">The shopping boutique booths and Kids Zone completed the Festival activities.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"> </p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">The children had a great time with their own lemonade stand, carnival games, </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">bungee run and open toddler bounce house. Umbrella tables inside the Kids Zone </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">made a comfortable place for parents to relax with friends and enjoy watching their</span></span>
<span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">children at play.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-size: medium;"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Guided by Fr. Moushegh Tashjian, the Festival was under the direction of Debbie </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Simonian, Chairman, Wayne Simonian, Vice Chairman, Varoujan Torikian, Treasurer, </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Joyce Kechian, Secretary, and Melissa Simonian, Art Director; and Committee </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Members: Christeen Amloian, Aret Aramian, Hovsep Bergciyan, Robert Kechian, Tony </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Markarian, Claudette Mekalian, Yn. Sylva Tashjian, Izzy Toumanian, Suren Toumanian, </span><span style="font-family: times new roman,times;">Susie Toumanian.</span></span></p>
<p style="text-align: left;"><span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">A huge thank you to our hard-working volunteers, guests, sponsors, and vendors for</span>
<span style="font-family: times new roman,times; font-size: medium;">your support!</span></p>
